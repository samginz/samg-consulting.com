About SG Consulting

SG Consulting helps growing organizations uncover hidden inefficiencies, redesign workflows, and build simple, reliable systems around the way they actually operate, not the way a slide deck says they should.

With experience spanning operations leadership, IT, and risk and insurance, we bridge strategy and execution. That means you get clear analysis, realistic recommendations, and hands-on support to implement changes without overwhelming your team.

What We Do

Operational Analysis

Department by department reviews to map workflows, quantify waste, and identify quick wins as well as structural issues. You get a clear picture of what is slowing you down and what to fix first.

Process Optimization

Redesign tasks, handoffs, and approvals to reduce duplication, close gaps, and improve accountability. We build simple SOPs and KPI dashboards so you can monitor performance and keep improvements in place.

Automation and Systems

Select and implement tools, dashboards, and automations that remove manual work without creating complexity. From spreadsheets and Access to cloud platforms and integrations, we work within the tools you already use where possible.

Hands-On Implementation

We do not stop at the recommendation stage. We work with your staff, vendors, and partners to put new processes into production, train users, and adjust until the system is stable and self sustaining.
